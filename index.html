Overview

This proposal covers a Purchase Order Management System for GSLF,GSPL and FGSPL. Both organisations operate under one login, each with its own GSTIN, CIN, logo and signatory, so a PO raised under either entity carries the correct legal identity on the printed document.

The system handles vendors, purchase orders, CGST/SGST/IGST computation with automatic intra-state and inter-state detection, PO status tracking from draft through paid, branded PDF generation, and Excel/PDF reporting — accessible from a browser on desktop or phone, installable as an app.

Why this build is lower risk for GSLF

This system will be built on Next.js, FastAPI and PostgreSQL — the same stack GSLF's Counselling & Student Lifecycle Management System already runs on. That means one technology footprint for your team to maintain, one deployment approach to learn, a shared authentication pattern, and the option to bring both systems under a single GSLF platform later without a rewrite. No second stack, no integration debt.
